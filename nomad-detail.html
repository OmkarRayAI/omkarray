Umbrian
Stone

14 DAYS
9.2 IDX

An exploration of silence and texture through Italy's green heart. We move through reclaimed stone structures, focusing on the intersection of ancient monastic life and contemporary brutalist intervention.

The Route
01—03

The Hermitage

Arrival in Perugia followed by a private transfer to the Eremo retreat. Early morning meditation and olive oil pressing workshops.

04—08

Spoleto Valleys

Hiking through the dense oak forests and visiting the Albornoz Fortress. Evenings focused on regional viticulture and local geometry.

09—14

Tiberian Silence

Final week at a converted 13th-century abbey. Architectural sketching sessions and slow culinary immersions.

Primary Residence

Vocabolo Moscatelli

A monastery reimagined by architect Jacopo Venerosi Pesciolini. Raw materials meet refined comfort.
