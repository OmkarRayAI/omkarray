Design Notes

Palette

Purple kraft paper, vermillion ink accent, deep aubergine for text. Inspired by risograph and letterpress printing.

Typography

DM Serif Display italic for brand presence. JetBrains Mono for data density — nutrition facts, SKU codes, and metadata.

DM Serif Display JetBrains Mono 400/700/800

Texture

WebGL fragment shader generates a real-time paper grain. Noise + directional fiber artifacts give the surface physicality without a texture image.
